Lessons Learned!

  • We didn't realize we needed flapjack until we did.
  • Flapjack handler didn't handle our throughput, rewrote it.
  • sensu-server not keeping up with rabbit queue. 15+ sensu-server processes.
  • sensu was OK for collecting metrics.
  • NOBODY EVER LOOKS AT DASHBOARDS.

Initially we wanted to punt remote sensus through squid proxy, PITA to implement, solved with flapjack.

As we grew, we had major issues with throughput in flapjack handler, we had to rewrite it.

We had a bug with one of our checks that cause us to need 15+ sensu server processes in some DCs.

Surprisingly using sensu client to collect our metrics was totally sufficient for our low metrics use case.
